Top three activities to do in Pune

Historical Marvel

Dive into Pune's rich history at Shaniwar Wada, the iconic fort that was once the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire. Wander through its majestic ruins and imagine the grandeur of its past.

Charming Vibes

Experience the cosmopolitan side of Pune in Koregaon Park. This area is known for its lush greenery, trendy cafes, and vibrant nightlife. It's the perfect spot to relax and soak in the city's youthful energy.

Architectural Splendor

Admire the stunning architecture of Aga Khan Palace, a landmark of national significance. This palace is not only beautiful but also played a crucial role in India's freedom struggle, serving as a prison for Mahatma Gandhi and other leaders.
